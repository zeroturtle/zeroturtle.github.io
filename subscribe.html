Technical Support

OPTIMUS offers tech support for customers with active subscription. During subscription period you will get:
  • free upgrades for subscription period (including new releases, bug fixes)
  • Online check version, also you will get an E-Mail when a new OPTIMUS version is available;
  • fast free technical support via e-mail for one year.
  • 30 days before a subscription expires, OPTIMUS will send an email notification to alert you to the fact that a new subscription will need to be purchased. An email notification will then again be sent 14 days before a subscription expires.
Please contact us via e-mail support@optimus.dp.ua or submit your question via Web Form. Usually we respond in 24 hours;

If your license has expired, you can easily get a new license by following these steps:
  1. Visit our subsciption page.
  2. Select Personal - Annual Subscription license.
  3. Fill in the details and complete your checkout.
  4. Check your email for your license confirmation.
  5. select Register and input your license details from your email.
  6. You can now continue using OPTIMUS with the same settings and setup.

Why renew your license?

  • The OPTIMUS Renewal Program Renewal price for next year is at an upgrade price equal to 25% of the list price (before your subscription expires).

  • The OPTIMUS Competitive Upgrade Program подволяет пользователям конкурирующих продуктов, оформить премиум подписку со скидкой 30% (требуется подтверждение собственности конкурирующего продукта).
