The OPTIMUS judging system was developed to enhance transparency, accuracy, and objectivity in evaluating Formation Skydiving, Artistic Events, Canopy Formation, and Wingsuit Flying Performance. Its goal is to make judging clear and understandable for athletes while providing a reliable tool for organizers.

  • For Judges:

    OPTIMUS simplifies the workflow, helps improve judging skills, and creates a foundation for knowledge sharing and professional growth among judges.
    • Streamline scoring
      focus on what really matters — the performance.
    • Accuracy you can trust
      Instant result calculation, team standings update automatically. The system checks for missing scores, inconsistent entries before final submission.
    • User friendly
      Multilangual interface; Color themes; Built-in documents editor; Customized data design.
    • Practice-anytime, anywhere
      Train your judging skills anytime — run simulations and test scoring without live events. Free for individual use

  • For Sports Federations:

    OPTIMUS provides standardized judging criteria and scoring templates across competitions. It is the easiest way to share information between judges, organizers, and audiences.
    • Consistent with FAI Rules
      All scoring templates follow official FAI and national regulations, ensuring compliance across disciplines and categories.
    • * Comprehensive Divepools Library
      Includes more than 30 divepool images from FAI, USPA, ФПСУ, DFV, AXIS — all ready to use out of the box;
    • Reliability
      Based on FirebirdSQL DB engine; cryptographic data protection.
    • Offline-Ready
      Internet connection is required only for result publication — judging can be performed entirely offline at remote dropzones.
