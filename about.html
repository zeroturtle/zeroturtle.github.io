What is OPTIMUS?

OPTIMUS is a all-in-one software suite developed for judging outdoor disciplines Formation Skydiving, Artistic Events, Canopy Formation. It also includes scoring functionality for Wingsuit Flying Performance. Its easy adapts for NAC rules. It offers low cost sulution for skydiving events organizers.

The Philosophy

The idea of OPTIMUS is driven by goal - increase Quality of life (QoL):

  • Zero-administration approach: No installation, No specialised equipment is required; The system is designed to be run by you, independently of us;
  • Flexability, Easy to Customize: ability adapt to individual requirements or terms of use;
  • User friendly: multilangual interface, detailed documentation, techsupport, regular updates garanted you always will be on board and use modern technical solutions and methods;
  • Competitive price: Availability for any budget: You set the subscription price yourself; Free for individual applying;

Subscription

A 1 year licence contains in each products subscription. Annual period is calculated as 365-days from subscription's date. After subscribing, we will send you an email with your personal licence and link to download OPTIMUS.

Individual Subscription is a free version of the OPTIMUS, allows our community:
  • train on your own;
  • make offline-testing;
  • online consultating;
  • Beta-testing programm give participants an oportunity to take part in product design;
  • free upgrades for 1 year (including new versions)
  • fast support by e-mail.
30 days before a subscription expires, OPTIMUS will send an email notification to alert you to the fact that a new subscription will need to be purchased. An email notification will then again be sent 14 days before a subscription expires. After your regular software subscription expires, you will be required to purchase a new 1-year subscription in order to continue having access to any new minor releases and online product support.
If the subscription was expired its will apply Freemium model when everything will work in single mode and you will receive Push-notification about it.

Looking to upgrade? Contact Our team to move to a more flexible license type.
