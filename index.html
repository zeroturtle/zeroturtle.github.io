OPTIMUS
Digital judgement and scoring system

About

All-in-one software suite developed for judging Formation Skydiving, Artistic Events, Canopy Formation and scoring for Wingsuit Flying Performance.

 Suitable for any size

Can be scaled as you like — from small events to international championships.

 Pay-as-you-go pricing

Avoid unnecessary expenses with our online license constructor — pay only for what you use.

 Share results instantly

Personalized pages, customizable colors, and DZTV broadcasts — all in one system for judging skydiving competitions.

Level up your judge workflow

Streamline scoring, reduce errors, and focus on what really matters — the performance.

Compatible with rules
Fully aligned with FAI and national judging standards;
Comprehensive Divepools Library
Includes more than 30 divepool images from FAI, USPA, ФПСУ, DFV, AXIS — all ready to use out of the box;
Built for everyone
Multilangual interface; Color themes; Built-in documents editor; Customized data design;
Accuracy you can trust
Instant result calculation and error detection — no more spreadsheets or manual scoring.
Practice-anywhere
Train your judging skills anytime — run simulations and test scoring without live events. Combined and consolidated methods to judgement.

The easist way to share information

Publish Results. Anywhere. Instantly.

Personalize event's One-pager
Includes web-embeddable results with styles and logo. Pre-built templates;
Keep all results synchronized
Publish events, photo, video, scores and details — all in one place; SSL secure.
DZTV integration
Display Video overlays with scores and rankings in real-time. QR-access to viewers;

Making history since 2008

From a small pet project to a cutting-edge judging platform — 15 years of evolution, innovation, and passion for precision.

2008

v.1.0 "OneScore"

Preliminary version of the system with a touch USB keyboard is released.

2011

v.2.0 "TwoScore"

Extended "Club mode".

2013

v.3.0 "Da-Vinchi"

Reengineering code to use a standard keyboard.

2014

v.3.1 "Darwin"

Personal screen to judge console added.

2016

v.3.2 "Eishtein"

Upgrade DevTools: Delphi 5->Delphi 7; Tools and components.

2018

v.3.3 "Tesla"

A check knowleage module has been added; new logo.

2019

v.4.0 "Aurora"

Localised Freefly functionality has been added.

2020

v.4.1 "Mosaic"

A "replay clips" module has been added.

2021

v.4.2 "Vanguard"

A upload video module has been added.

2022

v.4.3 "Fusion"

Add new disciplines Wingsuit Flying perfomance and Canopy Piloting.

2023

v.4.5 "Alchemy"

Implements the concept of an autonomous judge console.

2024

v.5.0 "Prometheus"

Refactoring DB data and scripts; Restyling Logo.

2025

v.5.2 "Artemis"

Based on FirebirdSQL DB engine; Upgrade DevTools: Delphi 7->Delphi 10.4.2; Upgrade tools and components;

2025

v.5.3 "Pegasus"

Unicode compatible; Web-site integration; online license generator.
